Frequently Asked Questions
How much does it cost to hire a developer?
Developer rates vary based on experience, skills, and project complexity. You can find developers in various price ranges, from $25/hour for junior developers to $150+/hour for specialized senior experts.
How do I know I can trust a developer?
All developers undergo a verification process, have visible work history, client reviews, and portfolio samples. Additionally, our payment protection ensures you only pay for satisfactory work.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
Our platform has a dispute resolution process. For milestone-based projects, you can request revisions before releasing payment. If issues persist, our mediation team will assist in finding a fair resolution.
Can I hire developers for long-term projects?
Yes, many clients establish ongoing relationships with developers they find on our platform. You can hire for one-time projects, part-time work, or full-time positions.
