Competition Overview
The AI Solution Challenge is our premier annual competition for developers to showcase their skills in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Participants will have 4 weeks to develop an AI-powered solution that addresses a significant real-world problem in one of the following domains: healthcare, education, sustainability, or accessibility.
Requirements
  • Solutions must be primarily AI/ML-focused and demonstrate innovation
  • Submissions must include source code, documentation, and a demo video
  • Teams can consist of 1-3 developers
  • All code must be original or properly licensed
  • Solutions will be judged on innovation, technical implementation, impact, and presentation
